About UFC

In 2010, the International Health, Racquet, and Sports club Association named Anytime Fitness the
fastest growing fitness club.
Anytime Fitness was the fastest growing health club franchise in 2014.
In 2015, Entrepreneur Magazine ranked Anytime Fitness first on its global franchise list.
Anytime Fitness is a subsidiary of Self Esteem Brands, LLC.
Forbes ranked the brand 14th as America’s most promising companies.
In 2014, Entrepreneur Magazine ranked Anytime Fitness number one on their franchise 500 list.
In 2015 and 2016, Anytime Fitness was ranked first on the publication’s top global franchises list.
In 2020 Anytime Fitness was ranked twenty-second on the publication’s franchise 500 list.
Anytime Fitness is ranked 9th in 2021 in Entrepreneur’s list of the Top Global Franchises.
Anytime Fitness has appeared for 14 straight years in the Top Global Franchises list and seven times in the top 10.
Anytime Fitness entered the Indian market in late 2012 and the Anytime Fitness Centers
are now operational and running successfully in 110 locations in India.
With an expertise of running gyms and feedback from more than 5,000 gyms worldwide,
Anytime Fitness deliver the success formula to its franchisees.
